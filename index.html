About Me

I am a mechanical engineering student at Northeastern University (Class of 2028) with a strong interest in building systems that combine mechanical design, embedded electronics, and rapid prototyping.

I began building engineering projects in high school and have continued developing hands-on experience through projects involving robotics, embedded systems, and experimental design. My work focuses on creating functional prototypes and interactive embedded systems while applying data-driven iteration and practical engineering problem solving.

Through these projects I have developed skills in mechanical design, microcontroller programming, CAD modeling, and rapid prototyping, along with experience using hand tools, SolidWorks, Arduino/ESP32 platforms, CNC Machines, 3D printers, and more.
